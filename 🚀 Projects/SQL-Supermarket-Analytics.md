### **1. ‡∏õ‡∏£‡∏∞‡∏™‡∏¥‡∏ó‡∏ò‡∏¥‡∏†‡∏≤‡∏û‡∏¢‡∏≠‡∏î‡∏Ç‡∏≤‡∏¢‡∏ï‡∏≤‡∏°‡∏õ‡∏£‡∏∞‡πÄ‡∏†‡∏ó‡∏™‡∏¥‡∏ô‡∏Ñ‡πâ‡∏≤ (Sales Performance by Product Category)**

    SELECT 
        product_line,
        SUM(total)  AS category_revenue
    FROM supermarket_sales
    GROUP BY product_line
),  grandTotal      AS ( -- step 2: ‡∏´‡∏≤‡∏¢‡∏≠‡∏î‡∏Ç‡∏≤‡∏¢‡∏£‡∏ß‡∏°‡∏ó‡∏±‡πâ‡∏á‡∏´‡∏°‡∏î

     t1.category_revenue,
